What Church of Christ the Resurrected One Intl. do?

PURPOSE: Nation transformation

CCROI will always believe and claim that the Philippines is a great nation, favoured and protected by God. As followers of Christ, we will continue to leave an impact in the lives of every Filipino. CCROI will continue to serve and live its mission, purpose, goal and vision to transformed lives, family, cities, the nation of the Philippines and influence different nations. Doulos will continue to banner that the Philippines is for Jesus!

With God's grace, everything that has been stolen by the enemy will be restored through the blood of Jesus. A life that is redeemed, restored and renewed all because of Jesus. We will help them unleash their God given potentials as an ambassador of Christ that follows His footsteps and become a blessing to their family, friends, and the people around them.

    - We help people find their way back to Jesus.
    - We help people to start up their new life.
    - We help people set their lives back into order.
    - We help people engage in God's mission.



GOAL: To make every believer a leader

As disciples of God, it is our great privilege to be entrusted with a ministry that can glorify the name of Jesus. As we bring out the best in each other in our cell groups, we are after teaching His words and the growth of one another. We will carry the right DNA as reliable disciples and witnesses of God's grace and divine power.

It is God who qualified us to continue the works of Jesus in raising up leaders of leaders. Doulos will ensure that the right DNA coming for Christ will be transferred from one generation to another as we lead people to embrace the calling of God in their lives as ambassadors and agent of change.
