Pastor Elizardo Carido

PS. Elizardo Carido

He is our pastor in Marila, Bulacan. Ptr. Elizardo Carido is an anointed preacher and leader who operates powerfully in the prophetic and has the rare ability to relate equally to both younger and older generations. He is committed to ‘speaking the language of the day’ by finding ways to present the unchanging, powerful message of Jesus Christ, in contemporary, relatable ways. He is passionate about empowering creatives to produce music and songs, and other artistic expressions, that are powerful and anointed in the message, but also innovative and contemporary in style, so that church continues to be attractive to and accessible for all people.

His life message is to continually ‘hunger’ for personal encounters with God” because it is in the place of encounter, that a person will find the acceptance, love, breakthrough, healing, empowerment, correction and direction that they need. His own relentless pursuit of God has resulted in the inspiring faith and bold exploits that characterise his leadership. He is also a man of great generosity, having proven first-hand, the faithfulness of God in areas of provision.
