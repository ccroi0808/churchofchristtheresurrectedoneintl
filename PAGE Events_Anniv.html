Doulos for Christ 32nd Anniversary Online Celebration


Doulos Celebrated its 32nd Anniversary Online, there is no reason for it not to happen because with Dod all things are possible.

s It has been three decades and two years since our dearest church was born. For 32 years, Doulos for Christ has remained faithful in its mandate of winning souls, making disciples and raising up leaders whom the multitudes could follow. It continues to stand as a testament of God’s faithfulness, goodness and covering upon His Church.

Last October 25, in the comforts of our homes, let us all together celebrate our dearest church’s 32nd anniversary with our family and friends - virtually. Let us look back and marvel how God has transformed thousands of lives through this church and press forward to the call of nation transformation, knowing that even amidst the current events, God is still sovereign and will continue to carry out His great and amazing plans for our lives, our families, our communities, this nation and the world.
