Doulos for Christ Campus Revolution


Changing this nation means changing one heart at a time. At G12PH Campus Leaders’ Revolution, we don’t just stir up the heart; we also awaken the mind and move the hands of our young people to bring back God’s original design in their lives. To cater more and more students who are willing and passionate to take part in God’s agenda of winning and discipling this nation.

We are moving on to greater heights, we are entering our new season, and we will not be silent about it! God's Word will not return to Him empty, but will accomplish its purpose in our lives and in our nation. Let the radical revolution God has begun continue to fire up in every campus and in every sector of our society. We will shout it out - the young generation belongs to Jesus! We will change the campus and the nation for God's glory!
